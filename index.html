Who We Are

Naura is a corporation that manufactures sodium-ion batteries for electric vehicles (EV). Currently EVs use a battery known as the lithium battery. Lithium batteries are very harmful to the environment, have long charging times, are inefficient in the winter, and have a high upfront cost.

Sodium-ion batteries have shorter charging times and are more efficient in the colder weather and climate. They are environmentally friendly to produce, abundant, inexpensive and sodium can be found around the world.
